Our Story

Founded in 2010, Rosey's Pasteries started as a small family business with a passion for baking. Our founder, Maria, began by selling her homemade bread at local farmers markets.

Today, we've grown into a beloved neighborhood bakery, but we still maintain the same commitment to quality, using traditional recipes and the finest ingredients.

Everything we make is baked fresh daily, with no preservatives or artificial additives.
